Container

* A `container` element wraps your content and sizes it according to [your breakpoint settings](https://github.com/fortitude/fortitude-sass/blob/master/app/assets/stylesheets/fortitude/settings/_defaults.scss#L130) * The `container-fluid` class works like `container` but with no breakpoints, resizing constantly. * The `container--fixed-top` modifier fixes your container to the top of the screen.
